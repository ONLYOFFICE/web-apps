Slide Master

The Slide Master allows you to edit the layout for all slides simultaneously, e.g., unify fonts or watermarks.

To enter the Slide Master mode:

  1. Go to the View tab.
  2. Click the Slide Master button on the top toolbar.

To manage master slides:

  1. Click the Add Slide Master button on the top toolbar of the View tab.
  2. The Master slides for each layout will appear in the slide list on the left. They are visible only to you and only in the Slide Master mode.
  3. Edit the slides the way you need them to look via the Home and Insert tabs.
  4. If you need more layouts, click the Add Layout button.
  5. Customize the slide layout. Click the Insert Placeholder button to add and edit specific content areas.
  6. Uncheck the Title and Footers buttons on the top toolbar if you do not need them.

To exit the Slide Master mode, click the Close Master button on the top toolbar, or go to the View tab and click the Normal button.
