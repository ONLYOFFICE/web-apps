Slide Master tab

The Slide Master tab in the Presentation Editor allows managing your master slides for editing the layout of all slides simultaneously.

Please note that the Slide Master tab will become visible only when you enter the Slide Master mode.

The corresponding window of the Desktop Presentation Editor:

Using this tab, you can:

  • add and delete master slides,
  • edit the layout and formatting of master slides,
  • rename master slides.
