Collaboration tab

The Collaboration tab in the Presentation Editor allows collaborating on presentations. In the online version, you can share a file, select a co-editing mode and manage comments. In the commenting mode, you can add and remove comments and use the chat. In the desktop version, you can only manage comments.

Using this tab, you can:

  • specify the sharing settings (available in the online version only),
  • switch between the Strict and Fast co-editing modes (available in the online version only),
  • add comments to your presentation and remove them,
  • open the Chat panel (available in the online version only).
